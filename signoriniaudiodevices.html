I’ve always enjoyed the creative side of Technical Engineering: developing products, designing solutions, and using my ideas to meet the needs of customers.
Every new device I create is driven by the desire to do more, combining technical skill with creativity. Always wanting to improve either someone else or my work and therefore improving my experience of doing what I love.

Custom boards for every project

I design dedicated PCBs for each project, ensuring every circuit is tailored to the exact requirements and features needed. This approach allows the design to be fully optimized for the dimensions and specifications of the project. Once finalized, the custom circuits are assembled into professionally manufactured printed circuit boards

Custom frontpanels

Each project comes with a custom-designed front panel, available in stainless steel or aluminum. Panels can be screen-printed or laser-etched in any required color to match the design

Design Philosopy

I want to build things the way they used to be built - with care, durability, and respect for the craft. My work pays homage to those legendary pieces of equipment that were built like tanks, modular, serviceable, and made with quality parts. I study how those machines were assembled because I want everything I create to carry that same spirit and to stand the test of time.

What I do

I’m not trying to compete with the big manufacturers, that will never be possible given their scale, and it’s not what I want to do.
I aim for the opposite: to make less, but make it in a way they never could. My focus is on creating something tailored exactly to your needs, something personal, built with care, that gives you the pleasure of owning a piece made just for you.
